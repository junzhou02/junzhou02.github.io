Jun Zhou

M.E. Student in Control Science and Engineering · Huazhong University of Science and Technology

I am currently a master's student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, where I am advised by Prof. Ye Yuan. My research lies at the intersection of control, optimization, and learning.

Broadly, I am interested in developing mathematically principled and reliable methods for the modeling, identification, and control of complex dynamical systems, especially in settings involving uncertainty, nonlinearity, and high dimensionality.

Starting in Fall 2026, I will join Columbia University as a PhD student in Electrical Engineering.

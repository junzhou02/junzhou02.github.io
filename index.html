Jun Zhou

M.E. Student in Control Science and Engineering · Huazhong University of Science and Technology

I am an M.E. student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, working at the intersection of dynamical systems, control, stochastic modeling, and Koopman-inspired analysis.

My research is shaped by training across mathematics and systems engineering, and by time spent at Manchester, Waterloo, and Toronto. I am especially interested in how operator-theoretic and optimization-based ideas can help us understand complex systems that are nonlinear, noisy, or infinite-dimensional.

Current interests: PDE control, stochastic system identification, spectral representations, and learning meaningful structure from data.
